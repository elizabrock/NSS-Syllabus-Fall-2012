LESS AND MORE

What?
  -A command to READ ONLY the files.  Good for reading information like READMEs and examples.
  -Common commands based on vi and more

The difference?
  -LESS allows you to move backwards, and doesn't load the whole file before displaying text
  -LESS is better

FIND OUT MORE
	-man less
	less --help
	-h or H while reading a file with less
